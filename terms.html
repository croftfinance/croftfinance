2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity Data: Full name, company name, project name
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, location
  • Financial Data: Funding requirements, facility amounts, project financials
  • Document Data: Uploaded documents, due diligence materials, legal agreements
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged through the portal, email correspondence
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, access timestamps (via Firebase analytics)

3. How We Collect Your Information

  • Direct interactions: When you register for portal access, submit contact forms, upload documents, or send messages
  • Automated technologies: Firebase authentication logs and Firestore timestamps
  • Third parties: Brokers introducing clients to Croft Finance

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Portal Access & Authentication: To verify your identity and provide secure portal access
  • Deal Management: To process your financing request, manage the 8-step closing process, and track documents
  • Communication: To respond to your enquiries, send notifications about deal progress, and facilitate advisor-client messaging
  • Compliance: To maintain records as required by applicable financial regulations
  • Improvement: To analyze portal usage and improve our services

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For individuals in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal information based on:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of our financing agreement
  • Legitimate interests: To manage client relationships and communicate about your project
  • Legal obligations: To comply with financial record-keeping requirements
  • Consent: Where required, we will obtain your explicit consent

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google (Firebase for hosting, authentication, database, and storage)
  • Email Services: EmailJS for sending notifications (encrypted in transit)
  • Capital Partners: Your project information may be shared with our capital partners under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Important Note: The identity of Croft Finance's capital partners is never disclosed as a condition of their participation.

7. Data Storage and Security

Storage Location: All data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which may process data in the United States, EU, or other regions. By using our portal, you consent to this transfer.

Security Measures:

  • Encryption at rest (Firestore and Storage)
  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Firebase Authentication with email/password protection
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Retention Period: We retain your personal information for the duration of our business relationship plus 5 years, in accordance with applicable financial record-keeping requirements. You may request earlier deletion where permitted by law.

8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at suzana.aleksic@croftfinance.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our portal uses essential cookies required for operation (authentication tokens, session management). We do not use marketing or analytics cookies without consent. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect portal functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
