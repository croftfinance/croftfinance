Who We Serve

Croft Finance operates at the intersection of private capital, family office balance sheets and institutional execution standards.

We partner with developers, operators and project sponsors that require decisive, bespoke capital for complex transactions. Mandates typically sit between $30 million and $250 million+, across real estate, infrastructure, natural resources and adjacent asset‑heavy sectors.

What We Do

High‑conviction structured financing for projects that sit outside standard bank underwriting.

Real Estate

Structured senior, stretch senior and mezzanine solutions for residential, hospitality and mixed‑use schemes.

Infrastructure

Project finance for social and economic infrastructure, with flexibility around security packages and covenants.

Natural Resources

Structuring capital around reserves, offtake and long‑term contracts in mining and energy.
