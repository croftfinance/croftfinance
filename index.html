Who We Are

Quiet capital.
Decisive execution.

Croft Finance operates at the intersection of private capital and structured finance. We work with developers, project sponsors, and project owners that require speed, discretion, and structural flexibility that institutional lenders cannot offer.

Every mandate is handled personally by Suzana Aleksić, Principal. We do not delegate. We do not hand off. From first conversation to funding disbursement, you work with one person who understands your project completely.
