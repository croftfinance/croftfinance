Who We Are

Quiet capital.
Decisive execution.

Croft Finance operates at the intersection of private capital and structured finance. We work with developers, project sponsors, and owners who require the speed, discretion, and structural flexibility that institutional lenders cannot provide.

Every mandate is handled personally by Suzana Aleksić, Principal. From first conversation to funding disbursement, you work with one person who understands your project completely — not a team of analysts.
