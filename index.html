Privacy Policy

Data Controller: Croft Finance, represented by Suzana Aleksić, Belgrade, Serbia.

Data Collected: Name, email, project information, messages, meeting requests, and document metadata.

Purpose: To provide secure client portal access and facilitate deal management between clients and Croft Finance.

Data Storage: Stored securely on Google Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit.

Retention: Data is retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 7 years for regulatory compliance, unless a deletion request is received.

Third Parties: Firebase (Google) for storage and authentication; EmailJS for notifications. No data is sold or shared.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact suzana.aleksic@croftfinance.com to exercise these rights.
